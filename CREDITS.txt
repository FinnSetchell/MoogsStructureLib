YungNickYoung (YUNG's API):
A big thanks to YungNickYoung for YUNG's API, from which this mod ports and adapts
several worldgen systems.

Ported / adapted from YUNG's API (LGPL-3.0):
- Enhanced terrain adaptation (beardifier): EnhancedTerrainAdaptation,
  EnhancedBeardifierHelper, BeardifierMixin
- Entity processing framework: StructureEntityProcessor, StructureProcessingContext,
  EntityProcessorMixin
- BlockStateRandomizer (inspired by YUNG's BlockStateRandomizer)
- Feature-cancelling mixin helpers: MixinUtils
- PieceLimitedJigsawManager (piece count limiting jigsaw manager)

Changes:
Most changes are noted at the top of each affected file. In general the ports are
reduced/trimmed to a self-contained MSL form. For example, the aquifer-override and
per-pool-element-override features of the original terrain adaptation are intentionally
omitted.
